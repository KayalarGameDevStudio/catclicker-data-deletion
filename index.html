CatClicker – Data Deletion Request

This page explains how you can request deletion of your CatClicker account data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to: kayalargamedevstudio@gmail.com

Subject: CatClicker Data Deletion

Please include your Player ID (visible in the app) and the email address you use on Google Play (optional).

Data that will be deleted

  • Cloud-saved game progress linked to your Player ID (e.g., inventory, upgrades, purchased items).

Data that may be retained by third parties

  • Google Play Games Services data (e.g., leaderboard entries) may be stored and managed by Google.
  • Advertising identifiers and ad-related data may be handled by ad providers according to their policies.

Processing time

We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days.
