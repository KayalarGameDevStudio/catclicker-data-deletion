CatClicker Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2025-12-17

1) Who we are

CatClicker is a mobile game published by Kayalar GameDev Studio ("we", "us").

2) What data we collect

Depending on your device and whether you use optional features (such as Google Play Games login), the app and integrated SDKs may collect and process:

  • User identifiers (for example, platform player IDs used for sign-in, cloud save, and leaderboards)
  • Player name (only if you sign in with Google Play Games and the name is available)
  • Purchase information (for example, to validate and restore in-app purchases)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address, typically used for ad delivery and measurement)
  • Device identifiers (such as advertising-related identifiers, used for ads and measurement)
  • App activity (such as ad interactions and basic in-app interactions)
  • Diagnostics (crash logs and performance data)

3) How we use data

  • App functionality: save and restore game progress, enable sign-in, leaderboards, and other online features
  • Advertising: show rewarded ads and measure ad performance
  • Analytics and diagnostics: improve stability and performance (for example, crash reporting)
  • Purchases: process, validate, and restore in-app purchases

4) Third-party services

CatClicker may use third-party services that process data under their own privacy policies, including:

  • Google Play Games Services (sign-in, leaderboards)
  • Google AdMob (rewarded ads, ad measurement)
  • Google Play Billing (in-app purchases)
  • Unity services (such as authentication and cloud save, if enabled)

5) Data sharing

We do not sell personal data. Data may be shared with service providers only to operate the app features listed above (for example ads, sign-in, purchases, cloud save, diagnostics).

6) Data retention

We retain data only as long as needed to provide app functionality and comply with legal or platform requirements. Cloud-saved game data is typically retained until you request deletion.

7) Security

Data is transferred over encrypted connections (for example HTTPS/TLS) where supported by the services we use.

8) Children

If the app is directed to children or includes children in its target audience, we commit to follow the Google Play Families Policy and configure our ad and data practices accordingly.
